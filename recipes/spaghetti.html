Spaghetti

Description

This is my go-to recipe for spaghetti and meatballs. Not only is it weeknight friendly – you can have it on the table in 45 minutes – but it is also picky eater-approved.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • Pasta
  • sauce

Directions

  1. Cook meat
  2. Boil pasta
  3. Add sauce to the meat
  4. Add pasta to meat
